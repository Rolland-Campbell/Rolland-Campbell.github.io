    Highland Authority

    Full-Stack (Vue / Node)

    Highland Authority is an application designed for spectators and athletes participating in Scottish Heavy Athletics Competition. The application is designed to give the spectator useful information about the different events and athletics going on around them. It helps spectators answers to some of the common questions we hear at events. Examples: How heavy is that weight?, What is the Sheaf? How do you score the toss of a caber? and many others. This application also allows athletes to create a user profile. Once created it allows the athlete to see all prior scores with our organization. The application also shows the athletes best personal record in each event (PR) in real-time. Spectators also have the ability to search athletes they may be interested in and see their scores, and other information the athlete decides to share on their profile. (COMING SOON) The final piece of the application is a real-time scoring system. This will eventually give the games judges the ability to record scores at the event, and the results will be saved to our database. Making competitions move smoother, and allow the ability to show rankings real-time at the current competition.
